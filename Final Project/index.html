Yedidim

In his youth, Weiner studied at the Karlin boys' school and later at the Karlin Yeshiva. After his marriage, he moved to Modi'in Illit, where he earned a living running the "Shehakol" store in Brachfeld.

When he began seeing civilians stranded on the roads with a flat tire, or trapped outside their home without a key, he founded the Yedidim organization, through which many volunteers provide service 24 hours a day.

In 2023 alone, Yedidim reported that its 65,000 volunteers rescued 1,842 children locked in cars, 249 animals trapped in cars and 10,000 drivers who had battery issues, flat tires or had locked the key in their cars.

Yedidim is a remarkable organization dedicated to providing essential support and assistance to children and families facing challenging circumstances in Israel. Founded on the principles of compassion, community, and commitment, Yedidim has made significant strides in addressing various social issues, particularly those affecting disadvantaged youth.

At its core, Yedidim aims to empower children and families by offering a range of services that cater to theiremotional, educational, and social needs. The organization operates various programs designed to uplift individuals,foster resilience, and promote personal growth. These initiatives are particularly crucial for children from low-income families, those experiencing familial disruption, or those who have been affected by trauma. By providing a nurturing environment, Yedidim helps instill hope and optimism in the hearts of these children, encouraging them to envision a brighter future.

One of Yedidim's most impactful programs is its mentorship initiative, where volunteers are paired with children in need of guidance and support. These mentors play a pivotal role in helping children navigate life's challenges, providing them with a listening ear, encouragement, and practical advice. This one-on-one relationship fosters trust and connection, enabling children to feel valued and understood.

In addition to mentorship, Yedidim also focuses on educational support. The organization recognizes that access to quality education is fundamental for breaking the cycle of poverty. Through tutoring sessions, workshops, and after-school programs, Yedidim ensures that children receive the academic assistance they need to thrive in school. The organization's dedicated staff and volunteers work tirelessly to create engaging learning environments that inspire curiosity and a love for learning.

Moreover, Yedidim extends its support to families as a whole. Understanding that a child's well-being is intricately linked to their home environment, the organization offers counseling services and family workshops. These programs aim to strengthen familial bonds, enhance communication skills, and provide parents with the tools they need to nurture their children effectively.

Yedidim's commitment to community engagement is also noteworthy. The organization actively collaborates with local schools, community centers, and other organizations to raise awareness about social issues and promote the importance of supporting vulnerable populations. By fostering a sense of collective responsibility, Yedidim inspires community members to get involved and contribute to positive change.

The impact of Yedidim is profound, as it transforms lives and instills hope in those it serves. Through its multifaceted approach, the organization has become a beacon of hope for many, demonstrating the power of compassion and community action. As Yedidim continues to grow and adapt to the evolving needs of society, its mission remains steadfast: to uplift, empower, and inspire the next generation, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to thrive and succeed.



Chaverim

Chaverim {Hebrew: >חברים, literally, "companions"}, also spelled Chaveirim, is an umbrella name for Orthodox Jewish volunteer organizations with locations all over the world; they provide roadside assistance and other non-medical emergency help at home or on the road.

All services are free.

The organizations are supported by local donations.Chaverim

Chaverim is a dynamic organization based in Modiin, Israel, dedicated to fostering a sense of community, support, and mutual aid among residents. Founded on the principles of friendship, compassion, and collaboration, Chaverim aims to enhance the quality of life for individuals and families by providing essential services and programs that cater to diverse needs.

One of the organization's core missions is to create a strong network of support for families and individuals facing various challenges, whether social, emotional, or financial. Chaverim recognizes that community ties play a crucial role in well-being and resilience. By promoting a culture of helping one another, the organization empowers residents to connect, share experiences, and build lasting relationships.

At the heart of Chaverim's activities is its volunteer program, which mobilizes community members to assist those in need. Volunteers play a pivotal role in offering companionship, support, and practical help, whether through home visits, organizing social events, or providing assistance during difficult times. This volunteer-driven approach not only benefits recipients but also fosters a sense of fulfillment and purpose among volunteers, creating a strong bond within the community.

Chaverim is particularly focused on supporting families and children. The organization runs various educational and recreational programs aimed at enriching the lives of young people. These initiatives include after-school tutoring, mentorship programs, and community events that encourage learning, creativity, and personal growth. By investing in the younger generation, Chaverim aims to instill values of kindness, empathy, and social responsibility.

In addition to its educational programs, Chaverim provides resources for families facing financial difficulties. The organization offers workshops on budgeting, job readiness, and skills development to help families achieve greater financial stability. By equipping individuals with the tools and knowledge they need, Chaverim empowers them to take control of their circumstances and improve their quality of life.

Meir Wiener, a dedicated professional within Chaverim, has been instrumental in driving the organization's initiatives forward. With his experience in community engagement and his commitment to social responsibility, he has played a significant role in enhancing the organization's impact in Modiin. Meir's passion for building connections and fostering a supportive environment aligns perfectly with Chaverim's mission.

The success of Chaverim is evident in the strengthened bonds within the Modiin community. By bringing people together and encouraging collaboration, the organization has created a network of support that enhances resilience and well-being. As Chaverim continues to grow and adapt to the evolving needs of its residents, its commitment to empowering individuals and families remains unwavering.

Through its various programs and initiatives, Chaverim serves as a vital resource for the Modiin community, promoting a culture of empathy, cooperation, and support. By uplifting those in need and fostering connections, the organization embodies the spirit of community, making a lasting impact on the lives of many.
